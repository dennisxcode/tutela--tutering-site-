# Tutela — Product Context

> Source of truth for *what Tutela is* and *who it's for*. Pairs with `DESIGN.md`
> (the look) and the founder's vault notes. Distilled from those — not invented.

**register: brand** — this is a marketing site; the design is the product.

## Product purpose
Small-**group** tutoring for the **Quebec secondary-school admission exams**
(升中考试 / 私立中学入学考试), taught by students from top Quebec schools
(incl. Collège Jean-de-Brébeuf) who **just sat those same exams**. The site exists
to make a Chinese-speaking parent in Montreal trust us enough to message on WeChat
and book the free trial. There is no checkout, no backend — the single conversion
is **starting a WeChat conversation**.

## Users
- **Primary:** Chinese-speaking **parents** of children in **primary grades 3–5**
  in Montreal / Quebec. Reading on a **phone**, often via a 小红书 (RedNote) link.
  State of mind: hopeful but cautious, comparing options, wary of hype. They decide
  on **trust and clarity**, not flash.
- Primary language **Simplified Chinese**; English is a courtesy view (中文 / EN).

## What we offer (verifiable — never inflate)
- Subjects: **Math, French, English** (the three core exam subjects).
- **Saturdays 9:00 AM–12:00 PM**, running through summer to ~September.
- Groups of **5–10** (limited spots). In-person + online, whichever fills.
- **$15 / hr / subject** — 1=$15, 2=$30, 3=$40 bundle. 1-on-1 by arrangement.
- **24/7 online question support** between classes.
- **First lesson free.** Practice exams modeled on recent content **coming soon**.

## Strategic principles
- Trust comes from **specificity + honesty**, not adjectives. Name the real schedule,
  the real schools, the real contests (AMC, Waterloo/CEMC), the free trial, the
  limited spots.
- Speak as **"we."** Dennis appears **only** at the contact/CTA point.
- The site must answer a parent's real questions: *what exactly will you teach my
  child, how, when, for how much, and why should I believe you.*

## Anti-references / anti-goals
- Not a generic edtech SaaS landing page (no "Elevate your learning", no dashboards).
- Not hype-driven cram-school marketing (no "#1", no fabricated results/testimonials,
  no stock tutor faces).
- Not a wall of text crammed onto one screen. Clarity through structure.

## Claim-safety (STRICT — enforced by tests; never violate)
- Math tutor: name **AMC + Waterloo/CEMC** only; **no superlatives** (no #1 / 第一).
- Location is **"确认中 / being finalized"** — never claim a named school or imply
  affiliation with any institution.
- Mock exams are **"即将推出 / coming soon."**
- **Never fabricate** results, enrolment numbers, testimonials, or photos of people.
